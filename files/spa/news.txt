14/09/2017

Takara will be one of the yachts of the Monaco Yacht Show

Monaco Yacht Show will be celebrated from 27th to 30th of September of 2017 in the port of the luxurious city of Montecarlo. Four exciting days where some of the best yachts of the world will be exhibited. One of them will be Takara, one of the best boats of the fleet of Charter&Dreams.
