Meridian Health has agreed to sell it's Michigan, Illinois and pharmacy operations to Wellcare Health Plans Inc, according to Crain's Detroit Business. The $2.5 billion deal is expected to close by the end of the year.

Founded more than 20 years ago, Meridian is Michigan's largest Medicaid health plan and is a major employer in downtown Detroit. It is unclear how the sale might affect Michigan operations. Wellcare is headquartered in Tampa, Fla.

Meridian consists of Meridian Health Plan of Michigan Inc., Meridian Health Plan of Illinois, Inc., and MeridianRx, a pharmacy benefit manager. Meridian expects to generate more than $4.3 billion in total revenue in 2018.

WellCare, which has 4.3 million Medicaid members as of the first quarter of this year, said the acquisition will bolster its Medicaid portfolio with new membership in several states. It reported income of $373.7 million on $17 billion in revenue in 2017.
