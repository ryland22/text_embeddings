Members of the editorial team from Flight International were present at the annual Aerospace Media Awards to accept the award for Best International Publication.

The Aerospace Media Awards honour individuals and publishers who have made a significant contribution to aerospace publishing.

BillyPix

"The Flight International team is delighted to have been recognised with this award, which underscores the magazine's status as the most authoritative aerospace weekly on the market," says editor Craig Hoyle.

"Along with our Flightglobal colleagues, we will continue to deliver the best coverage for our international audience in print, digital and online formats."

Individuals from the organisation were nominated for Best Business Aviation Submission, Best International Publication, Best In Depth Aviation Feature and The Bill Gunston Technology Writer of the Year Award.
