2888 Sheridan Place in Evanston: $3,100,000

Listed on Oct. 7, 2017

This 19-room home melds vintage elements with high-quality, modern updates and craftsmanship. It sits on a half-acre lot and features five fireplaces, six bedrooms, seven full bathrooms and a powder room. The DiGuilio chef's kitchen offers top-of-the-line appliances including a Sub-Zero glass-front refrigerator, Miele dishwasher and double ovens. The kitchen opens to a breakfast room with a cathedral ceiling and large family room, which overlooks the private bluestone patio. The second floor consists of four spacious en-suite bedrooms, the master suite with a fireplace, two master bathrooms and two dressing rooms. A finished lower level rounds out the home with a playroom, two offices, exercise room and a full bathroom.
