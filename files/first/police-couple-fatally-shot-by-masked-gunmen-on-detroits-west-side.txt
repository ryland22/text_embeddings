Police are looking for two masked gunmen after a double homicide on Detroit's west side early Wednesday morning.

The incident occurred at 12:30 a.m. on the 10000 block of Patton Street. A 48-year-old male victim reportedly answered a knock at the door, ran out of the house, and was chased and fatally shot on the front lawn, according to a statement from police.

A 45-year-old female victim was found fatally shot inside the family room, police said.

The man suffered a gunshot wound to the body and the woman suffered a gunshot wound to the head, and police said both were pronounced dead on the scene.

WDIV-TV reports the victims were engaged to be married.

Police said there were two suspects armed with handguns, wearing masks and driving a white Dodge SUV.
