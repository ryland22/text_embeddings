On Tuesday, March 27th, West Virginia Governor Jim Justice signed Senate Bill 451 into law. There will be a ceremonial signing of the bill today, at 11:30AM, in the office of the Governor in the West Virginia capitol building.

Senate Bill 451, sponsored by Senator Mark Maynard (R-6), expands hunting opportunities in West Virginia by legalizing Sunday hunting on all public lands.

Your NRA would like to thank the legislators, NRA members, and Second Amendment supporters who worked hard this session to pass SB 451, and Governor Justice for his signature. Stay tuned to www.nraila.org and your email inbox for further updates on issues affecting your Second Amendment rights and hunting heritage in West Virginia.
