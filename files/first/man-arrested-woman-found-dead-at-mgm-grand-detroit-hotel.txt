Detroit police have arrested a man suspected of fatally assaulting a woman at the MGM Grand Detroit hotel.

Police were called at 8 a.m. Tuesday about a man at the hotel acting hysterically, Detroit Police Officer Dan Donakowski said.

A woman was found dead in a hotel room. The man was arrested.

"It appears to be a domestic situation, a very tragic domestic situation," Detroit Police Assistant Chief James White told reporters.

No other details were immediately available.
